The Emergency Medical Service Locator

A Medical Servicing Web-App

Introductory to the EMS LOCATOR:

The EMS locator is a web-app that was created to help people in need of a medical service but need information where to get the best and most convenient services.

WHAT THE E.M.S LOCATOR DOES:

  • Makes it easier for one to identify the closest medical institution.
  • Gives clear details about the medical services at your current vicinity.
  • It avails important hospital-details such as their contacts and adresses.

Information: About it's Impact on service delivery.

The web-app cannot be held accountable for any withdrawal effect on other service delivery networks, as a matter of fact it's impact is generally positive because its role is more of a compimentary service.
